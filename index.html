Documentation for the Democraciv Discord Bot

Get Started

Built exclusively for Democraciv

The Democraciv Discord Bot, as the name implies, was built from the ground up for Democraciv.

Extensive features

All Laws, bills, motions & legislative sessions are being kept track of. Quick access to important Democraciv knowledge via Tags. Policital Party management & NPC system. Automatically mirrors important events to r/Democraciv. Supports the Speaker with powerful automation. On top of that, the usual bot systems like Starboard, selfroles, welcome message, server customization & smart utility commands.

Supercharged with Deep Machine Learning

Get answers to a legal question with the help of Machine Learning. Search through all bills & laws in seconds thanks to Natural Language Processing.

Integration with democracivbank.com

The deep integration with the Bank of Democraciv at https://democracivbank.com means you get DM notifications from the bot for new transactions, and access to all of your bank accounts right in Discord.
